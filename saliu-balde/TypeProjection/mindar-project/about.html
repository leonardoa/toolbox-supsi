ABC Dinamo TypeProjection © is an innovative tool designed for testing variable type characters in a dynamic and interactive manner. With the convenience of your smartphone, this tool allows users to project glyphs directly onto a wall, providing a real-time visualization of how the characters animate and adapt to different surfaces. The tool is specifically crafted to showcase the versatility of variable typefaces, allowing designers and enthusiasts to experiment with and assess the visual impact of characters in various contexts. ABC Dinamo TypeProjection © seamlessly merges technology and typography, offering a hands-on experience for exploring the expressive possibilities of variable fonts on any surface.
