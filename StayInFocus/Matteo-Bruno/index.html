-Stay in Focus-

"How many times, while working, do you still fall into the trap of notifications, sounds and the temptation of using the phone opening them and completely losing concentration on your goal? Well, this tool will send you animations every time you use your phone to remind you to come back and leave your phone aside. Need to go to the bathroom or take a short break? Set the timer to temporarily stop the tool!"
