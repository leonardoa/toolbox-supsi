Digital Daily Journal

Journaling is a practice that provides the opportunity to express deep thoughts and feelings related to what is happening, whether positive or negative, in one's life. It is defined as a 'diary' that accompanies daily life.

The fundamental factor of journaling is consistency; indeed, many people who practice journaling start but never complete it due to a lack of motivation or time. From this problem arises my tool, a digital daily diary that allows individuals to spend little time on the compilation.

The tool

Questions

Digital Daily Journal functions

With this tool, it is possible to dedicate a short amount of time to the daily diary compilation. Simply click the "Read question" button, and a question will be generated and read automatically. The response is done verbally, minimizing the efforts required by the person using it.

In the above-mentioned video, you can observe the person interacting with the tool. As mentioned earlier, the individual simply responds to the questions verbally. This way, the tool automatically transcribes what is said and records it in the archive, allowing for a chronological record of the provided responses.

Technologies Used

Here, you can see the technologies I used to develop the tool. Initially, I selected the voice that reads the question through the 'ElevenLabs' website. Subsequently, through the code, I implemented the automatic generation and reading of the question.

Secondly, I focused on configuring the microphone, allowing the person to respond verbally to the question. Thanks to this code function, the answer is automatically transcribed and recorded in the archive, eliminating the need for manual interaction with the device and ensuring a chronological record of previously given responses.

As a final step, I configured the saving of the answers provided by the individual under the section named 'Archive,' thereby creating a detailed chronology of the shared information.
